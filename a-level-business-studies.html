A Level Business Studies

A focused A Level Business Studies programme built around case studies, data response, and exam-focused evaluation. Students learn how to build high-mark answers with clear judgement, strong context and effective chains of reasoning. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete teaching pathway.

Maximum class size: 10 Case studies + data response Evaluation technique built-in
Included from Day 1: full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and access to past papers for the studentÄôs exam board.

How lessons run

  • Live weekly lesson: teacher-led explanation, worked examples, and guided application.
  • Data response practice: interpreting figures, selecting evidence, and drawing justified conclusions.
  • Evaluation training: build chains of reasoning, weigh trade-offs, and reach a clear judgement.
  • Exam technique: planning quickly, writing to the mark scheme, and timing under pressure.
  • Feedback: precise improvements focused on gaining marks.
