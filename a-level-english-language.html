A Level English Language

A structured A Level English Language programme that builds confident analysis, writing accuracy, and exam technique. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete teaching pathway.

Maximum class size: 10 Analysis + essay technique GCSE/iGCSE + A Level
Included from Day 1: full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and guidance mapped to major exam boards, plus access to past papers for the student’s board.

How lessons run

  • Live weekly lesson: teacher-led explanation, guided annotation, and worked model responses.
  • Skills practice: close reading, language analysis, and accurate academic writing.
  • Exam technique: timing, structure, planning, and mark-scheme language.
  • Independent work: targeted tasks between lessons to build confidence and fluency.
  • Feedback: clear, actionable improvements focused on gaining marks.

30-week overview plan (Sept to exams)

General structure. Exact ordering is adjusted by exam board and class needs.

Weeks Main focus Typical outcomes
1–5 Core language analysis skills + terminology refresh More precise annotation, better evidence selection
6–10 Text types, audience, purpose, and representation Stronger comparative points and clearer argument lines
11–15 Writing techniques: accuracy, style, structure, register More controlled writing with exam-appropriate tone
16–20 Language change, context, and key frameworks (board mapped) Confident use of concepts and structured explanation
21–25 Extended responses: planning, synthesis, evaluation Higher-quality essays with clearer judgement and coherence
26–30 Revision + timed past papers Exam readiness, speed, confidence, and consistency
