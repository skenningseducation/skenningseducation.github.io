A Level English Language

A structured A Level English Language programme focused on analysis, clear writing, and exam technique. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete pathway.

Included from Day 1: full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and access to past papers. Maximum class size: 10 students.

What we focus on

  • Accurate analysis using appropriate terminology
  • Evidence-led writing with clear structure
  • Comparative responses and evaluation skills
  • Exam technique: planning, timing, and targeting the assessment objectives

How lessons run

Weekly live teaching

  • Explicit modelling of high-mark responses
  • Guided annotation and planning routines
  • Targeted practice during the lesson
  • Regular checks for understanding

Homework + feedback

  • Weekly practice (short + extended)
  • Mark-scheme focused feedback
  • Redraft guidance to improve quickly
  • Timed exam practice throughout

Typical 30-week overview (support programme)

Adapted to the exam board and class pace, while keeping full coverage and exam readiness.

Weeks Focus Outcome
1–6 Foundations of analysis + core terminology Accurate annotation and clearer, structured paragraphs
7–12 Comparative analysis and viewpoints Confident comparison technique and stronger evaluation
13–18 Language, identity, and representation themes Better argument building and AO-targeted responses
19–24 Variation and data response skills More confident handling of datasets and evidence-led writing
25–27 Long-form responses and independent planning Faster planning and improved control under timing
28–30 Exam phase: timed papers + targeted improvement Timing strategy, exam confidence, final polish
