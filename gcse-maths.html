GCSE/iGCSE Maths

A structured Maths programme built around clear methods, lots of practice, and exam technique. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete pathway.

Included from Day 1: full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and access to past papers for the student’s exam board. Maximum class size: 10 students.

What we focus on

  • Confident methods for all high-frequency GCSE/iGCSE topics
  • Accuracy and “show your working” for maximum marks
  • Fluency with calculator and non-calculator skills
  • Exam technique: timing, common traps, and mark allocation

How lessons run

Weekly live teaching

  • Clear explanation + worked examples
  • Guided practice during the lesson
  • Checks for understanding throughout
  • Methods and “how to show marks”

Homework + feedback

  • Weekly practice set (mixed + targeted)
  • Corrections and model methods
  • Exam-style questions throughout
  • Timed practice for speed and accuracy

Typical 30-week overview (Sept to exams)

The exact order is adapted to the class, but full coverage is maintained.

Weeks Focus Outcome
1–6 Number, fractions/decimals/percentages, indices, standard form Stronger fluency and fewer errors in core skills
7–12 Algebra: manipulation, equations/inequalities, graphs, sequences Clear methods and improved problem setup
13–18 Geometry and measures: angles, area/volume, Pythagoras, trig basics Reliable geometry reasoning and accurate working
19–24 Statistics and probability + mixed problem solving Confident interpretation and complete solutions
25–27 Higher-mark topics and exam patterns (grade-building focus) Better performance on multi-step questions
28–30 Exam phase: timed papers + targeted improvement Timing confidence and consistent marks
