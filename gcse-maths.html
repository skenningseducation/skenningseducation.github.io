GCSE/iGCSE Maths

A structured GCSE/iGCSE Maths programme focused on clear methods, exam technique, and confidence under timed conditions. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete teaching pathway.

Maximum class size: 10 Method + accuracy Exam technique built-in
Included from Day 1: structured notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked solutions, and access to past papers for the student’s exam board.

How lessons run

  • Live weekly lesson: clear teaching with worked examples.
  • Guided practice: questions to build method and accuracy.
  • Exam technique: timing, marks-per-minute thinking, and showing working.
  • Homework: targeted practice between lessons.
  • Feedback: correction of errors and method improvement.

30-week overview plan (Sept to exams)

General structure. Exact ordering is adjusted by exam board and class needs.

Weeks Main focus Typical outcomes
1–5 Number, arithmetic, decimals, fractions, ratio Improved accuracy and reliable written methods
6–10 Algebra foundations (expressions, equations, graphs) Stronger rearranging, solving, and graph interpretation
11–15 Geometry and measures (angles, shape, perimeter/area/volume) Confident method selection and correct units
16–20 Probability + statistics + data handling More reliable exam responses and fewer interpretation errors
21–25 Higher-grade problem solving (as required by tier/spec) Better multi-step reasoning and method selection
26–30 Revision + timed past papers Improved timing, confidence, and exam technique
