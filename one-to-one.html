How one-to-one works

  • Personalised plan: we identify gaps, priorities and exam-board requirements.
  • Live teaching: clear explanation, modelling, and guided practice.
  • Exam technique: timing, structure, mark-scheme language, and avoiding common traps.
  • Targeted practice: questions matched to the student’s exact needs.
  • Feedback: precise actions to improve and gain marks.

Ideal for

  • Students who need rapid progress or confidence building
  • Higher-tier / top grade goals
  • Exam retakes or late starts
  • Students who prefer 1:1 pace and attention
  • Focused preparation for mocks or final exams
