FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about Skennings Education.

Which subjects and levels do you offer?

We offer Maths, English, and Computer Science at GCSE/iGCSE and A Level (English Language at A Level).

Is this supplementary support or main provision?

Both. Some students attend alongside school for extra support, while others use Skennings Education as their main teaching provision.

How big are the classes?

Maximum 10 students per class, so students receive attention and regular feedback.

What is included from Day 1?

Students receive full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and access to past papers for their exam board.

Which exam boards do you support?

The courses are taught in a way that works across the major exam boards, with clear signposting for board-specific requirements.

Do students get homework and feedback?

Yes. Students receive regular independent practice tasks and feedback focused on gaining marks and improving exam technique.

Can you help students who are behind or lack confidence?

Yes. We focus on clear explanation, structured routines, and step-by-step practice to rebuild confidence — especially in programming and exam responses.

How do we join?

Use the quick enquiry form to tell us the student’s level, year group and exam board (if known). We’ll recommend the best-fit group and next steps.
