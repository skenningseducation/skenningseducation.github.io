Frequently asked questions

What subjects do you offer?

We offer Computer Science, Business Studies, and English at GCSE/iGCSE and A Level.

Are lessons live or pre-recorded?

All lessons are live and teacher-led. Recordings are provided for small-group classes so students can review the content.

What is the class size?

Small-group classes have a maximum of 10 students to ensure interaction, feedback, and effective learning.

Do you offer one-to-one tuition?

Yes. We offer private one-to-one tuition for GCSE/iGCSE and A Level students who benefit from personalised support.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is per class. See the Prices page for current per-class pricing for group classes and private one-to-one tuition.

Are your teachers qualified?

All teaching is delivered by experienced teachers. School partnerships are supported by teachers with QTS and enhanced DBS checks.

Which exam boards do you cover?

Teaching is aligned to the student’s exam board, including AQA, OCR, Edexcel, and Cambridge (CAIE).

Can schools fund tuition?

Yes. We work with schools to provide remote teaching and tutoring, including support funded through Pupil Premium.

How do we get started?

Simply send an enquiry and we’ll discuss availability, suitability, and the best option for your child or school.
