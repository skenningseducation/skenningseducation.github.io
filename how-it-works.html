How it works

A clear, structured approach for GCSE/iGCSE and A Level students — whether this is supplementary support alongside school or the student’s main teaching provision.

What happens after you enquire

  • We confirm the student’s year group, exam board (if known), and current confidence.
  • We recommend the most suitable subject, level, and class slot.
  • We share the start date, the joining link, and what to prepare.

How weekly lessons run

  • Teacher-led explanation with worked examples.
  • Guided practice during the lesson (questions + feedback).
  • Exam technique built into the session (timing, mark schemes, common pitfalls).
  • Homework set each week to build consistency and progress.
  • Feedback designed to gain marks (what to improve and how).
  • Maximum class size: 10 students.

Day 1 resources included

On the first day, students receive a complete set of lesson notes in Word/PDF covering the major exam boards. We clearly indicate what applies to the student’s specific board, with exam-style questions and worked answers. Students also receive access to past papers for their exam board.

Subjects and levels

  • GCSE/iGCSE: Computer Science, Business Studies, English
  • A Level: Computer Science, Business Studies, English Language

Holiday masterclasses

During school holidays we also run focused Monday–Friday masterclasses (2 hours per day) to target weak areas and build exam confidence quickly.
