How it works

A clear, structured approach that works for GCSE/iGCSE and A Level students in Computer Science, Maths and English — whether this is supplementary support alongside school or the student’s main teaching provision.

Maximum class size: 10 students.

Day 1 resources included: students receive a full set of lessons in Word/PDF covering the major exam boards, clearly showing what they need for their board, with exam-style questions and worked answers. Students also receive access to past papers for their exam board.

Weekly live lessons

  • Teacher-led explanation and worked examples
  • Guided problem solving and practice
  • Regular questions and checks for understanding
  • Clear homework targets for the week

Homework and feedback

  • Weekly independent practice to build confidence between lessons
  • Regular exam-style questions to build marks under timed conditions
  • Feedback focused on gaining marks and fixing mistakes quickly

Exam technique and confidence

  • Timed questions and structured practice throughout the year
  • Mark-scheme awareness: what earns marks and what doesn’t
  • Common pitfalls and how to avoid them

What students receive

  • Live teaching every week
  • Structured lesson notes (Word/PDF)
  • Exam-style questions and worked answers
  • Past papers for their exam board
  • Support for supplementary or main provision students
