What “Remote Supply” means

Live teaching to your classroom

We teach your scheduled class live via video link. A member of staff supervises in the room while our teacher delivers instruction, questioning, and independent work.

  • Best for: short-notice absence, long-term vacancies, and hard-to-recruit subjects
  • Exam focus: GCSE/iGCSE & A Level routines and mark-scheme practice
  • Consistency: same teacher where possible for longer cover

What you need on-site

  • A cover supervisor / member of staff in the room
  • Teacher PC + projector/board + speakers (or a large screen)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Student books/devices if required for tasks

We provide the plan, resources, and live teacher. Your staff member manages behaviour and safeguarding in-room.
