For schools

Skennings Education works in partnership with schools to provide remote supply teaching and school-funded tutoring for GCSE/iGCSE and A Level students.

All teaching is delivered by fully qualified teachers (QTS) with current enhanced DBS checks.

Remote supply teaching

We provide reliable, high-quality remote supply teaching when schools are facing staff shortages, long-term absences, or recruitment challenges.

  • Live, timetabled lessons delivered remotely
  • Suitable for short-term cover or longer-term placements
  • Clear lesson structure and strong behaviour expectations
  • Full lesson resources and follow-up materials provided
  • Regular communication with heads of department or senior leaders

School-funded tutoring

Schools can fund targeted small-group or individual tutoring for students who require additional support, either alongside their main lessons or as a temporary alternative provision.

  • GCSE/iGCSE and A Level tutoring in Computer Science, Maths and English
  • Small groups or 1:1 delivery
  • Exam-board specific teaching and exam technique
  • Clear reporting on attendance, engagement and progress
  • Flexible timetabling to fit the school day

Pupil Premium funding

Our provision offers an effective and accountable use of Pupil Premium (PP) funding.

  • Targeted academic intervention with clear objectives
  • Small-group teaching to maximise impact
  • Structured resources and consistent teaching approach
  • Evidence of provision suitable for PP reviews and audits

Why work with Skennings Education?

  • All teachers hold QTS and current enhanced DBS
  • Strong subject knowledge and exam-board familiarity
  • Clear communication and professional accountability
  • Designed to integrate smoothly with existing school provision
