Holiday masterclasses

Intensive holiday programmes designed to strengthen key curriculum areas and exam technique. Delivered as 2 hours per day, Monday to Friday.

Format: 2 hours/day (Mon–Fri) · Small groups · Exam technique focus · Homework/practice tasks included.

Subjects: Computer Science, Maths, English (and English Language at A Level).

What we focus on

  • High-impact topics that drive marks quickly
  • Exam question technique and mark-scheme thinking
  • Targeted practice with feedback
  • Confidence under time pressure

Pricing (typical)

Pricing depends on level and group size. As a realistic UK benchmark for small-group specialist teaching, holiday intensives are commonly priced in the range of:

Level Schedule Typical price range (per week)
GCSE/iGCSE 2 hours/day · Mon–Fri £150 – £300
A Level 2 hours/day · Mon–Fri £200 – £350

Exact pricing and available weeks are shared by enquiry (so we can match the right group and focus areas).
