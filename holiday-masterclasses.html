Holiday masterclasses

Intensive short courses that run Monday–Friday, 2 hours per day. Designed to strengthen key curriculum areas and sharpen exam technique quickly.

2 hours/day (Mon–Fri) Small groups (max 10) GCSE/iGCSE + A Level
Best for: students who want a focused boost during half-terms/holidays, targeted help on weak topics, or structured exam-practice momentum.

How the week works

  • Day 1: baseline check + key method/knowledge reminders
  • Days 2–4: targeted teaching + guided practice + exam-style questions
  • Day 5: timed mini-paper + feedback + revision plan
  • Resources included: notes/worksheets and exam-style practice (Word/PDF)

Topics available

We choose a focus based on what the student needs most and what gains marks fastest.

  • Computer Science: programming practice (Python), algorithms, data representation, networks, security, exam technique
  • Maths: algebra, geometry, number/ratio, graphs, problem solving, exam timing
  • English: reading analysis, writing improvement, structure, exam responses, timing
  • English Language (A Level): language analysis, framework application, writing and exam technique

Pricing

Holiday masterclasses are an additional cost. As a typical UK market range for small-group specialist holiday tuition, a reasonable price point is:

Option What it includes Typical price
5-day masterclass 10 hours live teaching (2 hours/day), resources, exam-style practice, end-of-week feedback £149–£249 per student
Single day 2-hour focused session (topic-based) £35–£55 per student

Final pricing depends on subject, level, and class size. Availability varies by holiday period.
