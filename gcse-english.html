GCSE/iGCSE English

A practical GCSE/iGCSE English programme focused on reading skills, writing quality, and exam technique — with clear feedback that helps students improve quickly.

Included from Day 1: lesson notes (Word/PDF), model answers, exam-style questions and practice, and guidance tailored to the student’s exam board. Maximum class size: 10 students.

How lessons run

  • Live weekly lesson: skill teaching + modelling + guided practice.
  • Exam technique: how to structure answers and meet assessment objectives.
  • Independent work: short written tasks to build habits and confidence.
  • Feedback: precise improvements for content, structure, and language.

30-week overview plan

General structure. Exact texts and question styles depend on the student’s exam board.

Weeks Main focus Typical outcomes
1–5 Reading fundamentals: understanding and inference Clearer explanations and stronger evidence selection
6–10 Language analysis: methods and effect More precise analysis and improved paragraph structure
11–15 Writing skills: narrative/description/persuasion (board dependent) Better structure, range of vocabulary, and control of tone
16–20 Comparison and evaluation questions Stronger organisation and higher-level critical thinking
21–25 Timed practice + exam planning routines Improved speed, clarity, and consistency
26–30 Revision + past papers Exam readiness and confident performance
