GCSE/iGCSE English

A GCSE/iGCSE English programme focused on reading analysis, writing quality, and exam technique. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete teaching pathway.

Maximum class size: 10 Reading + writing Exam technique built-in
Included from Day 1: structured notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with model responses, and access to past papers for the student’s exam board.

How lessons run

  • Live weekly lesson: teacher-led modelling of analysis and writing.
  • Guided practice: structured responses with clear success criteria.
  • Writing improvement: sentence craft, vocabulary choice, structure, and control.
  • Homework: targeted reading/writing tasks between lessons.
  • Feedback: practical edits and next-step targets to gain marks.

30-week overview plan (Sept to exams)

General structure. Exact ordering is adjusted by exam board and class needs.

Weeks Main focus Typical outcomes
1–5 Core reading skills (inference, evidence, explanation) More precise answers with better quotation selection
6–10 Language analysis (methods, effects, writer’s purpose) Clearer analytical paragraphs and stronger terminology use
11–15 Structure and comparison (as required by exam board) Better organisation and more coherent comparison responses
16–20 Writing: narrative / descriptive / transactional (as required) Improved structure, control, and marks for content/accuracy
21–25 Higher-mark strategies + timing + common pitfalls More consistent grades through technique and planning
26–30 Revision + timed past papers Exam readiness, confidence, and improved timing
