GCSE/iGCSE English

A practical English programme focused on high-mark responses, confident writing, and exam technique. Suitable as supplementary support or as a complete pathway.

Included from Day 1: full lesson notes (Word/PDF), exam-style questions with worked answers, and access to past papers. Maximum class size: 10 students.

What we focus on

  • How to write high-mark answers (structure, evidence, explanation)
  • Analysis skills: techniques, meaning, effect and author purpose
  • Writing skills: clarity, accuracy, and shaping responses for the mark scheme
  • Exam technique: timing, planning, and common pitfalls

How lessons run

Weekly live teaching

  • Explicit modelling of high-mark responses
  • Guided annotation / planning routines
  • Targeted practice during the lesson
  • Regular checks for understanding

Homework + feedback

  • Weekly practice (short + extended)
  • Mark-scheme focused feedback
  • Redraft guidance to improve quickly
  • Timed exam practice throughout

Typical 30-week overview (Sept to exams)

The exact order is adapted to the exam board and class needs, while keeping full coverage.

Weeks Focus Outcome
1–6 Core analysis and paragraph structure Clearer, more convincing answers with evidence and explanation
7–12 Language techniques, inference, and evaluation style questions More accurate interpretation and stronger marks on common question types
13–18 Writing focus: planning, shaping, accuracy, and control Improved writing quality and structure under time pressure
19–24 Mixed reading + writing exam patterns Better timing and confidence on full sections
25–27 Higher-mark responses and refinement (grade-building focus) Stronger top-band technique and fewer repeated mistakes
28–30 Exam phase: timed papers + targeted improvement Exam confidence, timing strategy, and consistent marks
